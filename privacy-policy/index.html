Introduction

Welcome to Kameti. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when using our app, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile details when you create an account
  • Committee Data: Information about committees you create or join, including member details and payment records
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and authentication tokens

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Usage data (app features used, time spent in app)
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Advertising ID — used by Google AdMob to serve personalised advertisements

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our application
  • Create and manage your account
  • Process and track committee payments
  • Send you notifications about committee activities
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Serve relevant advertisements through Google AdMob to support the free app

Advertising

Kameti is a free application supported by in-app advertising. We use Google AdMob to display ads. The following ad formats may appear:

  • App Open Ads — displayed when you launch or resume the app
  • Native Ads — displayed inline within the committee list

Google AdMob may use your Advertising ID and device information to show you personalised ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

Opting out does not remove ads from the app — you will still see ads, but they will not be personalised based on your interests.

Google's privacy policy governs how AdMob handles your data. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted open-source backend platform. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure authentication methods
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication requirements

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database storage — supabase.com/privacy
  • Google Sign-In: Account authentication — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob: In-app advertising — policies.google.com/privacy

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising ID and device information for ad delivery and measurement
  • Supabase — Your account and committee data for storage and cross-device sync
  • With other committee members — limited to committee-related information only
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With your explicit consent

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of personalised advertising (see Advertising section above)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Ads served through Google AdMob are configured for a general audience. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy
