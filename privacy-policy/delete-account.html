Account Deletion Request

We respect your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your Kameti account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Profile Information: Your name, email, phone number, and profile picture
  • Committee Data: Committees you created and your membership records
  • Payment Records: All payment history associated with your account
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and tokens
  • App Preferences: All settings and preferences

Data Retention Period

Your data will be deleted within 30 days of your request. Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes for up to 90 days before permanent deletion.
