# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 24, 2024

## Introduction

Committee - Payment Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for authentication)
- Display name (optional)

### Committee Data
- Committee names and details you create
- Member names and payment records
- Payment dates and amounts

### Device Information
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the app service
- Sync your data across devices
- Send important notifications about your committees
- Improve our app and user experience

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in:
- **Firebase Cloud Firestore** (Google's secure cloud database)
- **Local device storage** (for offline access)

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure authentication (Firebase Auth)
- Access controls and authentication

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Export your committee data
- Opt out of notifications

## Data Deletion

To delete your data:
1. Go to Profile in the app
2. Select "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion

All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
